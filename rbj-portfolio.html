The Project

From Empty Space
to Premier Venue

OHM Lounge started as an underdeveloped space with raw potential. The challenge was a ground-up redesign of the interior — transforming the environment, the brand, and the atmosphere into a venue that could attract top talent and host unforgettable nights.

The result was a fully realized nightlife destination that hosted concerts, live events, and private performances — documented here from first day of construction through its final curtain call.
