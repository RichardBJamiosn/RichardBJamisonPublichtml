Background

A Life Lived
in Rhythm

I started playing drums at age 11. By 17 I was already working professionally — blues bands, wedding bands, all across New York. Music was the first language I learned to speak fluently.

"In 2000 I earned an Associates Degree in Music from SUNY. Since then I've toured with rock, reggae, and jazz projects — and played in Fusion Jazz, Punk, and Hardcore bands."

While living in San Diego I taught drum set at A Reason To Survive, a non-profit for terminally ill children. A ten-year hiatus followed. I've now spent the last year rebuilding — chops, meter, feel. A drum method video series is currently in production.
